Common questions

        


        

          

            What is a Steam ID?
            
It is the permanent 17-digit number connected to a Steam account. Unlike a display name or custom profile address, it never changes.

          

          

            Does this work with private profiles?
            
The account can often still be found, but Steam may hide details such as playtime, location, profile text, groups, and inventory.

          

          

            Do I need to sign in?
            
No. Search without connecting your Steam account or sharing any login details. Only information that Steam already makes public can be displayed.
